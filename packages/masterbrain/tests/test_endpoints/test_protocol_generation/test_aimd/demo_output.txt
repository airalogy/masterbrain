## Experimental Steps

{{step|seed_solution_preparation,1}} 种子溶液的合成.

Add 50 μL of HAuCl4 (20 mM) to 4.75 mL DI water.
Add 100 μL of sodium citrate solution (10 mM).
Note: Avoid storing the mixture above 35°C, as sodium citrate may reduce Au³+ to unwanted gold nanoparticles. Store in a 3-8°C refrigerator after addition.
Add 100 μL of NaBH4 (0.1 M) solution under vigorous stirring.
Note: NaBH4 powder should be dissolved in cold water (3-8°C) to prepare 0.1 M solution. Some experimenters suggest letting freshly prepared NaBH4 solution stand for 20 minutes in a 3-8°C refrigerator before use.
Stir for 2 minutes.
The mixture should change from yellow to brown, indicating seed solution formation.
Let the seed solution sit at room temperature for 2 hours.
Note: This step is for NaBH4 decomposition. Keep the seed solution at 25-30°C for at least 2 hours.

{{step|growth_solution_preparation,2}} 生长溶液的合成.

Place 108 mL of CTAB (0.025 M) solution in a container.
Note: CTAB powder usually dissolves at higher temperatures, such as 60°C. Ensure the CTAB solution cools to room temperature before proceeding.
Add 1.5 mL of HAuCl4 (20 mM) to the CTAB solution while gently shaking.
While shaking, add 600 μL of 0.1 M NaOH solution.
Add 54 μL of KI (0.1 M), continue gentle shaking.
Finally, add 600 μL of 0.1 M AA (ascorbic acid) solution while shaking.
Note: Some experimenters consider continuous shaking during steps b-e crucial.
The solution should change from yellow to colorless.
Note: If the final solution does not turn colorless (e.g., appears pale pink), discard it and repeat steps a-f.

{{step|triangle_nanostructure_synthesis,3}} 三角形金纳米片的合成.

Add 100 μL of seed solution (from step 1) to 900 μL of growth solution (from step 2).
Gently shake for 3 seconds.
Add 9 mL of growth solution to the mixture.
Note: Alternatively, you can add 1 mL of the mixture from step b to 9 mL of growth solution.
Shake the combined mixture for 4 seconds.
Mix with 92 mL of growth solution.
Note: The 92 mL mentioned in the literature may be a printing error, as the remaining growth solution is approximately 98 mL. Using either 92 mL or 98 mL of growth solution is acceptable in this step.
Gently shake the final mixture for 6 seconds.
Let the solution sit for 30 minutes.
Note: During this period, avoid moving the bottle. If the ambient temperature is below 20°C, you can gently place the bottle in a 30°C oven to prevent CTAB precipitation.
The solution color should deepen to purple, indicating the formation of gold nanosheets.
Note: The deep purple color from this step comes from approximately 10 nm spherical gold nanoparticles, not triangular nanosheets.
Let the nanosheets precipitate for 24 hours.
Note: The optimal precipitation time is 24-48 hours. Less than 24 hours will result in low nanosheet yield, while more than 48 hours may introduce impurities like spherical nanoparticles.
Carefully pour off the supernatant after the nanosheets have precipitated.
Note: Be careful when pouring off the supernatant to avoid disturbing the precipitate. Nanosheets often adhere to the bottle walls, forming a nearly invisible thin layer that becomes visible only after all the supernatant has been removed.
Resuspend the nanosheet precipitate in 10 mL DI water.
Note: For long-term storage, it is recommended to store the nanosheets in 10-25 mM CTAB solution to prevent further growth.
The final solution should appear green.
Note: If the solution does not appear green (e.g., appears pale purple or pink), restart the entire process.

{{check|solution_color_check,1}} 溶液颜色检查.

Before proceeding, ensure that the solution has turned colorless (for growth solution) and green (for final solution).

{{check|precipitation_check,2}} 沉淀检查.

Before proceeding, ensure that the nanosheets have precipitated properly and the supernatant has been carefully poured off.
